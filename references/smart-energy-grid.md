
# Smart Energy & Grid

## Grid Integration Protocols
- IEEE 2030.5 / SEP 2.0 (REST/TLS, California Rule 21 mandate, DER management, demand response, EVSE, pricing)
- OpenADR 2.0a/2.0b IEC 62746-10-3 (VTN→VEN signals: DR, price, load control; XMPP/HTTPS/SOAP transport)

## Data Models & Advanced Metering
- IEC 61968 / 61970 CIM (Common Information Model, UML-based, ADMS/DMS/SCADA/OMS)
- ANSI C12.18 / C12.19 / C12.22 (optical port, meter data tables, North American AMI)
- DLMS/COSEM cross-ref (primary global meter protocol, OBIS codes)
- Wireless M-Bus / OMS cross-ref (EN 13757-4, pairs with DLMS)
- SPINE / EEBUS (SHIP protocol over WebSocket, JSON-LD, EV charger + heat pump + solar interop)

## Power Line Communication
- G3-PLC ITU-T G.9903 / PRIME ITU-T G.9904 (IPv6 6LoWPAN over PLC grid, Linky/France, PRIME/Spain)

## Regional Metering
- SMETS2 / GBCS (UK Smart Metering Equipment Technical Specification v2, DLMS/COSEM over ZigBee SEP 1.x, DCC national hub, HAN: ZigBee)

## EV Charging & Battery
- IEC 61851 (EV conductive charging, PWM pilot signal Mode 1-4)
- EU Battery Passport EU 2023/1542 (mandatory 2027, EV/LMT/industrial >2kWh, SoH/SoC/cycle count, LwM2M integration)
